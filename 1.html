Barfi

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of full-fat milk powder 1 cup of condensed milk
  • 1/2 cup of ghee (clarified butter) 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cardamom powder (optional, for flavor)

Recipie

  1. Combine 2 cups milk powder, 1 cup condensed milk,
  2. 1/2 cup ghee, and 1/2 cup milk, cook until thick,
  3. add cardamom, set in a tray, refrigerate, and cut into pieces for homemade barfi.

Gulab-Jamun

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk powder, 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup ghee (clarified butter), A pinch of baking soda
  • 1/4 cup milk (approximately), Oil or ghee for deep frying

Recipie

  1. Mix 1 cup milk powder, 1/4 cup all-purpose flour, 1/4 cup ghee.
  2. a pinch of baking soda; add milk to form a dough, shape into balls, deep-fry until golden.
  3. soak in sugar syrup for delicious gulab jamun.
